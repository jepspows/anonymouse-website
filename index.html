📜 THE LEGEND OF ANONYMOUSE

In the shadowed corners of the Great Kitchen, where cheese flows like golden rivers and crumbs fall like manna from above — there lived a mouse unlike any other.

His name was whispered in the walls: ANON.

By day, a humble mouse. By night... a shadow that struck fear into the hearts of the CHEESE CARTEL.

The Cartel ruled the kitchen with an iron paw. Led by the tyrant KING RATTUS and his lieutenant SLICK WHISKERS, they hoarded the finest aged gouda and taxed every crumb that fell.

No mouse dared defy them. Those who tried... vanished into the ventilation shaft.

But Anon was different. He had the POWER. The power of the BLOCK.

⚔️ THE BATTLE OF THE WHEEL

Anon discovered the ancient art of CHAIN MANIPULATION. By weaving strands of digital gold, he could move faster than any cat, hide deeper than any shadow, and strike without warning.

His mask: a crumb of parmesan, hardened by the fires of the oven.

His weapon: a toothpick dipped in ghost pepper extract — the BURNING STRIKE technique.

The final battle took place atop the GREAT CHEESE WHEEL of the pantry — a wheel so vast it could feed a thousand mice for a thousand moons.

Anon faced Rattus and Slick in a duel of wits, speed, and CHAIN POWER. With one swift move, he redirected the Cartel's own cheese supply back to the colony.

The wheel was FREE. The mice were FREE.
