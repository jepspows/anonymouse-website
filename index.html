About Anonymouse

Anonymouse is a collection of 10,000 uniquely generated mouse living on the blockchain. Each mouse is 100% on-chain — the art, metadata, and traits are stored directly in the smart contract.

Every Anonymouse is procedurally generated from a pool of traits — backgrounds, fur colors, eye styles, headgear, and more. No two mouse are alike.

Stake your Anonymouse to earn tokens. The more you hold, the more you earn. Build your colony and reap the rewards.
